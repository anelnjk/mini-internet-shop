Science Story unites the energy of the present moment and the optimism promised by a sustainable future. The collection is built around modern and experimental garments made with innovative new textiles. The looks are elevated by unexpected design elements like flashy cutouts and adjustable details and tamed by a calm and refined palette of sky blue, pure white and mint green. Key pieces include bright white wide-leg jeans with a removable double waistband, a cropped collared shirt with an oversized breast pocket and a cuffed tracksuit. Bold, dynamic accessories inject a thrilling flash of colour and vitality. A few of our favourites are a chunky necklace made of interconnecting orange swirls and embellished with rhinestones, block-heeled acid yellow strappy sandals and double-framed blue sunglasses.Every innovation is a step forward. Every positive change is an action of progress. Together, we have the power to shift the narrative of fashion toward a more sustainable future.


HAVE YOUR ALWAYS BEEN INTO FASHION?
”Yes, thanks to my mum and my dad. My dad was a fashion designer, so I saw a lot of wax fabrics growing up – my mum was always asking for African dresses in colourful patterns. That helped me a lot to find my style in terms of colour. I love to play with different styles when I’m travelling, or changing my haircut!”
